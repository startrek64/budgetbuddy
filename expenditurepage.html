Expenditure Page

Welcome to the expenditure page.

To enter an ammount that you have spent, click on Declare Expenditure.

This will link you to a form regarding the purchase details and the amount.

This amount will link up to your very own financial history table.

To track your financial history, simply click on Financial History.
